Privacy Policy

This policy is a placeholder for this demo and should be reviewed before going live.

JW Motors respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect the personal information you share with us, in line with the Privacy Act 2020.

1. What we collect

When you contact us or submit a form on this website, we may collect your name, phone number, email address and details about your vehicle and the work you need. We only collect what we need to respond to your enquiry and carry out your job.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide a quote
  • To book and carry out work on your vehicle
  • To contact you about your booking or vehicle
  • To keep records of work carried out

3. Sharing

We do not sell your information. We only share it where necessary to complete your job — for example, ordering parts — or where we are required to by law.

4. Storage & security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and to protect it from loss, misuse or unauthorised access. Website enquiries are delivered to us by email via our form provider.

5. Your rights

You may ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request that we correct it. Contact us using the details below.

6. Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at wilcodealdirect@hotmail.com or 027 476 2404.
