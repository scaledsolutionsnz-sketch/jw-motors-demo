Privacy Policy

How we handle the information you share with JW Motors.

Last updated:

JW Motors respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we use it. (This is placeholder content for the demo and should be reviewed before going live.)

1. What we collect

When you submit an enquiry or feedback form, we collect the details you provide — typically your name, phone number, email address, and any message or vehicle details you include. We don't collect more than we need to respond to you.

2. How we use it

  • To respond to your enquiry, prepare a quote, or arrange a service or sale.
  • To follow up about your vehicle or request.
  • To improve the service we provide.

We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for unrelated marketing without your consent.

3. How it's stored

Form submissions are delivered to us by email via a third-party form service. We keep your details only as long as needed to deal with your enquiry and meet any legal obligations.

4. Sharing

We only share your information where necessary to provide the service you've asked for (for example, a finance or parts provider) or where required by law.

5. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can ask to see the personal information we hold about you and request corrections. To do so, contact us using the details below.
